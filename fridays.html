Updates

21 March 2011
We have all been following the tragic events in Japan caused by the Great Tohoku Earthquake. 10M-member Keisuke Ishihara has been working day and night to organize Harvard for Japan Week, which started today and runs all week. This Friday (3/25) is the Mendelssohn, Brahms & Tchaikovsky Japan Benefit Concert featuring card-carrying pro violinist Ryo Goto (Harvard '11). We want to encourage everyone to attend the concert, and we want to attend ourselves, so this week we will not be having a potluck, but instead will just be hanging out at 10MIYTBNCCO after the concert, starting around 10pm. The concert is open to the public, and tickets are available at the Harvard Box Office and at the door). Hope to see you there!

The first potluck last week was a great success, thanks to those who came out!
